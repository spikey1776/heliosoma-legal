PRIVACY POLICY FOR AURASYNC

Effective Date: June 2026

AuraSync ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to providing high-quality space weather biophysical synergy tracking, cosmic guidance, and planetary advisory tools. We treat user privacy with the utmost integrity.

1. Data Storage & Privacy Protection

By default, your birth details, coordinates, and daily alignment history logs are stored locally in your device's sandboxed storage. If you choose to enable AuraSync Cloud, your settings and daily logs are synchronized securely to our servers. Your secret passkey is fully encrypted. We do not sell or share your personal data, settings, or logs with advertising networks, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

2. Google Gemini AI Integration & Disclosures

To provide daily personalized guidance forecasts, the AI Biophysical Synergy Reports, and the Interactive Somatic Advisor (Cosmic Oracle) chat answers, the App securely transmits specific, non-personally identifiable inputs (such as your zodiac sign, general birth details, occupation, personal life focus, and chat text logs) to Google Gemini API (Google Cloud). No personally identifying information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or exact GPS coordinates) is ever sent. All transmissions are encrypted using standard HTTPS protocol.

3. Full User Data Deletion Rights

In compliance with privacy regulations (GDPR & CCPA) and App Store Guidelines, you retain absolute control over your data. You can delete all locally cached data at any time via the Systems Control panel. If you are signed in, deleting your account instantly and permanently purges your profile records and logs from our servers.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA & Age Gate)

AuraSync incorporates an explicit age verification gate. The application is intended only for individuals aged 13 or older. We do not knowingly compile information from minors under 13.
